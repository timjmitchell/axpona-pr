Did you find your audio-nirvana this weekend? With so many fabulous headphones, music enthusiasts, and gearheads in attendance, this year's EarGear at Axpona would have been an easy spot to find your own slice of Hi-Fi Heaven. All of whom we'd like to thank for working so diligently alongside ourselves in putting together a fantastic weekend of listening, conversation, and discovery! [@ZMFHEADPHONES](https://www.head-fi.org/members/598595/) [@Audeze](https://www.head-fi.org/members/124348/) [@MezeTeam](https://www.head-fi.org/members/437885/) [@iFi audio](https://www.head-fi.org/members/361812/) [@Sennheiser](https://www.head-fi.org/members/146072/)

Thank you for your time, for sharing your stories and your favorite songs. We we're proud as always to demo our amazing new [HeadAmp CFA3 Headphone Amplifier](https://www.headamp.com/products/headamp-cfa3) & [HeadAmp Blue Hawaii Special Edition](https://www.headamp.com/products/blue-hawaii-se), alongside [Dan Clark Audio](https://www.headamp.com/collections/dan-clark-audio/headphones).  
  
That said, though, we'd love to hear from everyone who had a chance to attend. What did you enjoy, what did you listen to this weekend, and ultimately, what put a smile on your face?
