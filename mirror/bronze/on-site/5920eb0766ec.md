#  Frequently Asked Questions
[Who can come to AXPONA? ](https://axpona.com/faq/)
**AXPONA is open to the general public.** Whether you are a lifelong audiophile, a newcomer curious about high-end sound, or a music lover looking for the best way to enjoy your favorite albums, you are welcome to attend.
All attendees must purchase a ticket for admission.
[Where does AXPONA take place? ](https://axpona.com/faq/)
The Renaissance Schaumburg Hotel & Convention Center, 1551 N Thoreau Dr, Schaumburg, IL, 60173
[What are the hours for AXPONA? ](https://axpona.com/faq/)
Friday, April 10, 2026 10:00 AM – 6:00 PM
Saturday, April 11, 2026 10:00 AM – 6:00 PM
Sunday, April 12, 2026 10:00 AM – 4:00 PM
[What is included with my ticket? ](https://axpona.com/faq/)
Your ticket includes access to the exhibits including listening rooms, the Exhibit Hall including the Record Fair, Ear Gear Experience, the Car Audio Showcase as well as educational seminars, live music, special events.
[What is a Gold Pass? ](https://axpona.com/faq/)
The Gold Pass is the ultimate AXPONA experience. Your Gold Pass includes: official AXPONA T-shirt, access to the AXPONA Gold Lounge, VIP check-in, Coat Check for coats and event purchases, refreshments, WiFi, and Happy Hour with wine and beer on Saturday, April 11 from 5:00 – 6:30 PM. Plus access to the Gold Hospitality Suite in the Hotel Tower, with seating, water, snacks and a restroom.
[How do I get to AXPONA? ](https://axpona.com/faq/)
Whether you are arriving by train, plane or automobile, a full set of directions is available at [here](https://axpona.com/hotel-travel/).
[Is there parking available? ](https://axpona.com/faq/)
We will have complimentary self-parking at the Renaissance Schaumburg Hotel & Convention Center. Valet parking is available for a fee of $25 per day.
[I have arrived at the Convention Center, what do I do now? ](https://axpona.com/faq/)
The first thing you need to do is go to REGISTRATION on the first floor and get your badge which will give you access to all that AXPONA has to offer!
Then, pick up a copy of the EVENT GUIDE which will help you plan your time at the show.
[Where can I find the exhibitors Hi-Fi listening rooms? ](https://axpona.com/faq/)
There are exhibitor listening rooms in the Convention Center on Levels 1 and 2 and in the Hotel Tower on Levels 1, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, 11, 12, 14, 15, and 16.
[Do I need an appointment to go into the listening rooms? ](https://axpona.com/faq/)
No, during show hours you can visit with any of the exhibitors in the listening rooms.
[What if a door is closed to a listening room? ](https://axpona.com/faq/)
As long as you are visiting during show hours and you knock, the exhibitor will let you in.
[Will there be live music performances? ](https://axpona.com/faq/)
Yes! You can find our concerts for this year [here](https://axpona.com/concerts/).
[How much does it cost to attend? ](https://axpona.com/faq/)
Tickets vary in price. Check out our [Buy Tickets page](https://axpona.com/register/) for a complete list.
[How do I know which dealers, manufacturers and brands will be at the show? ](https://axpona.com/faq/)
Prior to the show you can view the [exhibitor list](https://axpona.com/exhibitors/) on our website.
While you are at the show the event guide that has an exhibitor index as well as exhibitor profiles. Event guides can be picked up at registration.
You can also download our mobile app to plan ahead and access the information while you’re at the show.
[Do you have a Mobile App? ](https://axpona.com/faq/)
Yes! Download **myShowApp** from your app store on any iOs and Android Device. Open the app and select “Find an Event” and search for AXPONA. Create an account and build your own personal AXPONA itinerary.

[Where can I find a restroom in the Convention Center and Hotel? ](https://axpona.com/faq/)
