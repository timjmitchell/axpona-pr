## Business Overview

**AXPONA** is the premier annual event serving the high-end audio market. Over 750 high-fidelity audio brands demonstrate products and play music in 225 listening rooms, using hotel rooms, suites and meeting rooms. Exhibits showcase products in the Expo Hall, Ear Gear Expo, Car Audio Expo, and Record Fair.

Three days of educational seminars and Master Class sessions, two nights of live concerts, and an annual pre-show industry networking reception for trade only that attracts hundreds of exhibitors, international business professionals and industry press.

Exhibitors display thousands of high-end audio products, primarily focused around speakers and sound systems, featuring equipment and components, such as cables, tubes, amps, turntables, reel-to-reel, vinyl, headphones, hi-res streaming systems, car stereos and accessories.

AXPONA is held in a unicorn event venue that fits its unique show needs perfectly, and that no other audio show can match. The Renaissance Schaumburg Hotel & Convention Center, the AXPONA home since 2018, is an upscale, all-inclusive complex, including a 500-room hotel, a large ballroom, 48 meeting rooms and large suites, a 100,000 square foot pillar free exhibit hall, nonunion labor rules, and multiple F&B outlets, all under one roof, plus 1,800 free onsite parking spaces.

The venue is located only 14 miles, a 20-minute ride, from Chicago O'Hare Airport, the airline travel hub of North America, making it easily accessible for domestic and international travelers. Several nearby hotels are used for overflow sleeping rooms. AXPONA occupies the entire facility. Renaissance management wishes to keep AXPONA under contract long-term, as it is one of their most profitable annual tenants.

The **14th annual** AXPONA Event was held April 11–13, 2025 in Chicago, Illinois at the Renaissance Schaumberg Hotel & Convention Center.

## AXPONA

**AXPONA** (*Audio Expo North America*) is the premier event for Consumers, Trade and Press to experience the world's high-end audio brands.

AXPONA was launched in 2010 as a small local audio show in Jacksonville, Florida. The Event was acquired by JD Events in 2014.

Now in its **15th year**, AXPONA has been built into the largest and most important high fidelity audio equipment tradeshow in North America and is increasingly recognized as the premier high end audio event in the world. AXPONA serves the luxury audio market, defined as products that cost more than $5,000 (or $10,000 per pair for loudspeakers), which grew **12%** in 2023 to $2.8 billion in total sales, according to Futuresource Consulting. Industry dynamics are highly resilient with spending growing at a CAGR of 9.9% to 2023.

The high-end audio category has been further bolstered by a resurgence in vinyl record demand and production. Vinyl sales have seen 19 consecutive years of growth, outpacing CD sales in 2022, along with renewed growth in the manufacturing and sales of turntables and other high-end audio equipment. Per Futuresource, the US is emerging as the key driver of future growth. With economic policies increasingly favoring wealth creation, high-end spending is expected to gain momentum, further boosting demand for premium audio. The luxury audio market is expected to reach **$4.3 Billion** by 2028.

## Transaction

### Reasons for Selling

Joel Davis is the sole Managing Member of the Company — he owns the majority of the Company along with a group of friends & family and trade show industry veterans. Joel has full authority to buy and sell Company assets.

The Event was acquired by JD Events in 2014 from an individual.

JD Events is a tradeshow incubator. They have been building and selling events since 2002. They are a small boutique shop that launches, builds, proves viability and profitability, and then finds the right strategic owner with the resources to grow their properties long-term. AXPONA's growth and future potential is exceeding what JDE is capable of managing. The dedicated full-time AXPONA staff will remain with the show. JDE's management and operations team is available to support, consult and guide the new owner through the next event cycle or two, if needed, to ensure a seamless transition. This is the JDE business model. The most important customer to JDE is the company that buys their tradeshow. They want a happy buyer who will say good things about JDE in the tradeshow industry.

JD Events believes that AXPONA is gaining momentum now, due to the strengthening of the audio market, and is considering moving the Event into an organization that has a strategic fit and/or the vision to take AXPONA to the next level. JD Events is happy with the performance of the Event and the positive cash flow it delivers but will consider a divestiture of the asset for the right buyer and desirable transaction terms.

### Financial Reporting

The Company operates on a December 31 calendar year end. All numbers presented for the years ended 2018 through 2025 are actual results. 2026 is based on the most current forecast.

The Company uses accrual-based accounting methods. Event revenues are accrued on the balance sheet as deferred revenue until the Event is held, at which point it is recognized as revenue on the profit and loss statement. Expenses incurred relating to future Events are accrued as prepaid expenses until the Events are over, at which point they are recognized as expenses on the profit and loss statements.

The Company is a Limited Liability Company (LLC) filed in the state of Delaware.
