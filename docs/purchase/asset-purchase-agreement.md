## ARTICLE III — PURCHASE PRICE AND PAYMENT

### 3.1 Purchase Price

The aggregate purchase price for the Assets shall be determined as follows (the **"Purchase Price"**):

**If the Closing occurs before the 2026 Event (April 10, 2026):**

(a) **Base Purchase Price:** The 2025 Event EBITDA of $781,484, multiplied by the Multiple;

(b) **Acquired Profits:** An additional payment equal to the positive difference between (i) the actual 2026 Event EBITDA and (ii) $781,484, multiplied by the Multiple, payable on or before July 15, 2026 (the **"Second Payment"**).

**If the Closing occurs on or after the 2026 Event:**

(a) **Purchase Price:** The actual 2026 Event EBITDA (forecast: $943,175), multiplied by the Multiple.

### 3.2 Payment at Closing

At the Closing, Buyer shall pay the Base Purchase Price (or Purchase Price, as applicable) as follows:

(a) **Cash at Closing:** Ninety-five percent (95%) of the applicable Purchase Price, payable by wire transfer of immediately available funds to an account designated by Seller;

(b) **Escrow Deposit:** Five percent (5%) of the applicable Purchase Price, deposited with the Escrow Agent pursuant to the Escrow Agreement.

### 3.3 Net Deposits Adjustment

(a) **Calculation.** The Purchase Price shall be adjusted at Closing by the **"Net Deposits"** amount, calculated as:

> **Net Deposits = Deferred Revenue − Prepaid Expenses**

as of the Closing Date. If Net Deposits is a positive number (Deferred Revenue exceeds Prepaid Expenses), the Purchase Price payable at Closing shall be reduced by the Net Deposits amount (reflecting that Buyer is acquiring a net obligation). If Net Deposits is a negative number, the Purchase Price shall be increased accordingly.

(b) **Estimated Net Deposits.** Not later than five (5) business days prior to the Closing Date, Seller shall deliver to Buyer a good-faith estimate of Net Deposits as of the anticipated Closing Date (the **"Estimated Net Deposits"**), together with reasonable supporting documentation.

(c) **True-Up.** Within ninety (90) days after the Closing Date, Seller shall prepare and deliver to Buyer a final calculation of Net Deposits as of the Closing Date (the **"Final Net Deposits"**). If the Final Net Deposits differs from the Estimated Net Deposits:
- (i) If the Final Net Deposits exceeds the Estimated Net Deposits, Seller shall pay to Buyer the difference within fifteen (15) business days;
- (ii) If the Estimated Net Deposits exceeds the Final Net Deposits, Buyer shall pay to Seller the difference within fifteen (15) business days.

(d) **Dispute Resolution.** If Buyer disputes the Final Net Deposits calculation, it shall notify Seller within thirty (30) days of receipt. The parties shall attempt to resolve any dispute in good faith. If unresolved within fifteen (15) days, the dispute shall be submitted to a mutually agreed independent accounting firm, whose determination shall be final and binding.

### 3.4 Purchase Price Allocation

The Purchase Price shall be allocated among the Assets in accordance with Section 1060 of the Internal Revenue Code and the Treasury Regulations thereunder (the **"Allocation"**). The parties shall negotiate the Allocation in good faith and shall each file IRS Form 8594 consistently with the agreed Allocation.
