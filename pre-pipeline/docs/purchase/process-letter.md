# AXPONA OM Process Letter

> **From:** Corporate Solutions, LLC
> **Date:** January 20, 2026
> **Re:** Sale of AXPONA by JD Events

---

Corporate Solutions, LLC ("CS") exclusively represents JD Events (the "Company") in the sale of its hybrid event: **AXPONA** in the audio industry (the "Business").

You have provided us with a signed Non-Disclosure Agreement and you have recently received the Offering Memorandum for the Business.

The purpose of this letter is to provide you with a timeline to participate in this exclusive and private offering.

## Proposal Requirements

Interested participants must provide Corporate Solutions with a written proposal (LOI or IOI) to acquire the Business **no later than February 12**. However, early submission is encouraged so that there is enough time to properly consider. The proposal must be signed by an officer or other authorized representative and include the following information:

1. Your intention to acquire the Business assets of the Company.
2. Purchase price and specific terms of payment.
3. Sources of funding for completing the acquisition.
4. Timeline of significant milestones including: due diligence start date, due diligence ending date, date to provide initial draft of the purchase agreement and closing of the transaction.
5. Escrow requirements including amount and term.
6. Confirmation of an adjustment to the amount paid to the seller at the closing for "Net Deposits". Net Deposits is defined as deferred revenues actually received less prepaid third-party expenses and employee compensation expenses actually paid for the Event edition to be held after the closing.
7. Your intentions for the Company in terms of continued involvement in running the Business post-closing and the term of such continuation.
8. Your intentions to hire the three staff that work on the Business.

## Timeline

- **February 12** — Deadline for written proposals (LOI/IOI)
- **February 17** — Notification of proposal acceptability

Proposals must remain in effect until February 17.

## Communication Protocol

- The owner and CFO are willing to participate in telephone conversations and virtual meetings to discuss the Business and respond to questions.
- **All questions regarding this process must be directed only to Corporate Solutions.**
- Under no circumstances, unless otherwise indicated, should there be any direct communication with the Company, its owner or its employees relating to this proposed transaction.
