**_AXPONA 2026 ANNOUNCES LIVE CONCERTS FEATURING AMBER RUBARTH AND CAELAN CARDELLO_**
**SCHAUMBURG, IL – February 5, 2026** – AXPONA (Audio Expo North America), the premier event for audiophiles and music enthusiasts, is thrilled to announce two extraordinary live performances during its 2026 show at the [Schaumburg Convention Center](https://www.schaumburgconventioncenter.com/). On Friday, April 10 and Saturday, April 11, guests will enjoy two world-class concerts by acclaimed artists Amber Rubarth and Caelan Cardello.
**Caelan Cardello – Friday, April 10, 2026 | 8:00 PM
Sponsored by Wire World Cable Technology and WDCB FM**
On Friday, the AXPONA stage welcomes Caelan Cardello, a rising star in the modern jazz scene. A virtuosic pianist, composer, and bandleader, Cardello has earned acclaim for his expressive improvisation and sophisticated compositions that merge classic jazz tradition with contemporary innovation.
A recipient of the BMI Future Jazz Master Award and a finalist in the American Pianists Awards, Cardello has performed at world-renowned venues such as Jazz at Lincoln Center, Smalls, and Dizzy’s Club. His 2025 debut album _Chapter One_ showcases his dynamic range and creative voice, earning praise from both critics and peers. Audiences at AXPONA can expect a performance that embodies technical mastery, emotional depth, and a fresh perspective on modern jazz.
“I find it wonderfully satisfying and enjoyable to make music with pianist, Caelan Cardello. He possesses such savvy of our music,” said jazz- great bassist Rufus Reid.
**Amber Rubarth – Saturday, April 11, 2026 | 8:00 PM
Sponsored by Wire World Cable Technology and WDCB FM**
AXPONA’s concert series presents Amber Rubarth on Saturday night, an award-winning singer-songwriter and multi-instrumentalist celebrated for her soulful voice, poetic lyricism, and cinematic sound. Known for her intimate storytelling and acoustic artistry, Rubarth’s music bridges folk, Americana, and indie influences, earning critical acclaim for albums such as _Wildflowers in the Graveyard_ and _A Common Case of Disappearing_.
Her songs have been featured in major films and she has shared stages with artists including Jason Mraz, Emmylou Harris, and Kenny Loggins. At AXPONA, Rubarth will deliver a captivating performance blending heartfelt originals with inspired arrangements that highlight her warm vocals and intricate musicianship.

**ABOUT**
AXPONA (Audio Expo North America) is North America’s largest event for audiophiles, manufacturers of high-end audio equipment, musicians and music lovers. The three-day experience features 200+ high fidelity listening rooms, providing guests the enviable opportunity to experience the newest technology in high-end consumer audio products. Attendees are encouraged to go from room to room and sit, listen and compare the various systems. The Expo Hall and Ear Gear Experience play host to thousands of products and accessories from cables to headphones to LPs and SACDs. The Hi-Fi Car Audio Showcase brings together the best in high-fidelity car audio systems, featuring a thrilling Sound Quality (SQ) competition.
Beyond the exhibits, AXPONA includes live musical performances and educational seminars with industry experts and demonstrations.
