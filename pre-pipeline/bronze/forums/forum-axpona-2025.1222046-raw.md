> I'll second someone elses post that still not see and hear everything in three days. It kind of depends on how much time you spend and where. I have met people who have done the whole show im a single day but I imagine they're just dashing from room to room and zipping through the Expo Hall and Ear Gear.  
>    
>  Friday is usually pretty busy but you can still find a seat in most every room. Saturday is always a madhouse and some rooms have a wait or even a line just to get a seat. Sunday is typically a ghost town for some reason, especially if the weather isn't great. Sunday is usually the day I revisit and spend a good amount of in rooms that interested me the previous days.  
>    
>  Starting at the top and making your way down us a common strategy. Lines at the elevators, especially in Saturday, can be a bit ridiculous.  
>    
>  If you're hungry and don't want to wait in line or wait a long time for or at your table, there's decent food options at the concession area at the back of the Expo Hall and it's usually not that busy.
