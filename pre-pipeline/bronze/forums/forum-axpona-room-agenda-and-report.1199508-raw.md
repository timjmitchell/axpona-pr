> A word about the following room reviews. Having been fortunate enough to have a nice system of my own that I have tuned and perfected over the years, my listening bar is set kind of high. That being said, the hardware vendors of these rooms are often veterans of audio show combat and they know what they are doing for the most part- witness room treatments, speaker placement and system synergy. They have this expensive chance to show the attendees of the show what they and their systems can do.   
>  Also this year marked the presence of many more than usual video based reviewers. Seemed like every room had dude with a professional looking phone / camera / grip / extender contraption. Stakes were high for these rooms to sound great- and most that I attended did just that.
